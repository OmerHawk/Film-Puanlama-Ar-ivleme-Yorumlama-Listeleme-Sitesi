The Narrative

In a decaying metropolis where digital echoes replace human connection, a veteran film restorer discovers a mysterious reel that shouldn't exist. As he delves into the celluloid mystery, the lines between his reality and the film's haunting frames begin to blur into a visceral noir odyssey.
